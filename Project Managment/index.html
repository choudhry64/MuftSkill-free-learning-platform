PROJECT MANAGEMENT

What is Project Management?

Project Management is the process of planning, organizing, and overseeing a project to achieve specific goals within a set timeline and budget. It involves managing tasks, resources, and teams to deliver results, like launching a product or completing a campaign, while handling risks and changes.

How to Learn Project Management?

Start with a basic understanding of concepts like planning and teamwork; no degree is required, but a background in business can help. Key skills include Team Collaboration, Project Planning, Scheduling, and Agile/Scrum Methodologies —we’ll teach you these here! Soft skills like communication, leadership, and problem-solving are also essential. Learn through free online courses on platforms like MuftSkills, and practice with small projects, like organizing an event. Explore certifications like CAPM or Scrum Master to boost your credibility.

3.Agile and Scrum Methodologies for Project Management

Agile and Scrum are flexible project management methods that focus on breaking work into smaller, manageable tasks, with regular reviews to adapt to changes quickly. Watch the tutorial below to learn Agile and Scrum Methodologies for Project Management!

Why Learn Agile and Scrum Methodologies?

Adapt to changes and feedback during the project.
Collaborate closely with your team in daily stand-ups.
Perfect for managing dynamic, non-tech projects.

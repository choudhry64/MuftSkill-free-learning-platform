BUSINESS COMMUNICATION

WHAT is Business Communication?

Business Communication is the process of sharing information effectively within a professional setting to achieve business goals. It includes verbal, written, and visual methods like emails, reports, presentations, and meetings, ensuring clarity and professionalism in interactions with colleagues, clients, or stakeholders.

How to Learn Business Communication?

Start with a basic understanding of professional etiquette and practice key skills. Focus on Professional English Speaking by joining speaking clubs or watching tutorials on platforms like YouTube (e.g., channels like English Addict with Mr Steve). Learn Email Writing by studying templates and practicing concise, polite emails—resources like Coursera have free courses. Master Report Writing through online guides or templates on sites like HubSpot, focusing on structure and clarity. Develop Presentation Skills by using tools like PowerPoint and practicing with free courses on Udemy or YouTube (e.g., Shaw Academy). Build soft skills like active listening and clarity through daily practice and feedback. Consistent practice with real-world scenarios, like drafting emails or presenting ideas, will help you improve quickly.

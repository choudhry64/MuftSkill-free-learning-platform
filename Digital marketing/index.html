DIGITAL MARKETING

WHAT is Digital Marketing?

Digital Marketing is the promotion of products or services using online channels like search engines, social media, email, and websites. It focuses on reaching customers digitally, analyzing campaign performance, and optimizing strategies to boost engagement and sales.

How to Become a Digital Marketer?

Start with a basic understanding of marketing concepts; a degree in marketing, communications, or business helps but isn’t mandatory. Learn hard skills like SEO (optimizing websites for search engines), Google Ads (running paid ad campaigns), and Email Marketing (engaging customers via emails)—we’ll teach you these skills here! Soft skills include creativity for crafting campaigns, analytical thinking to interpret data, and communication to collaborate with clients. Practice by running small campaigns, explore free tools like Google Analytics, and take beginner-friendly courses on platforms like MuftSkills. Build a portfolio with projects to land roles in e-commerce, agencies, or startups.

FINANCE AND BUDGETING

WHAT is Finance and Budgeting?

Finance involves managing money, including earning, spending, saving, and investing, to achieve personal or business goals. Budgeting is a key part of finance, where you plan your income and expenses to ensure you live within your means, save for the future, and avoid debt. Together, they help you take control of your financial health.

How to Learn Finance and Budgeting?

Start with a basic understanding of money management—no degree is needed for personal use. Learn skills like budgeting, expense tracking, tax planning, and basic investing through free online resources. Focus on practical skills: use tools like Excel or apps (e.g., YNAB, Mint) to track expenses, create budgets, and plan savings—we’ll guide you with these tools! Develop habits like setting financial goals, reviewing spending weekly, and learning from mistakes. Watch beginner-friendly YouTube tutorials (e.g., from channels like The Financial Diet or Graham Stephan) for step-by-step guidance. Practice with small, real-life examples, like planning a monthly budget, to build confidence. Explore more free courses on MuftSkills to keep growing!
